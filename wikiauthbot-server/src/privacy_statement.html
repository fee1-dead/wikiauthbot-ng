Privacy Statement of WikiAuthBot2

Information we collect

Upon authenticating to your Wikimedia account via the /auth command, we store your Discord user ID and your Wikimedia CentralAuth ID.

This information will be retained indefinitely unless you use /deauth on all servers that use WikiAuthBot2, after which the information will be deleted.

We use this information such that Discord identities can be linked to Wikimedia identities for the purpose of identification, server moderation, authentication to channels with privileged access, etc.

How we protect your information

Regular queries refer to queries issued by a user on a server about themselves or other users via the use of commands including /whois, /revwhois, etc, as well as access to the data through a web API provided to limited trusted applications that access data on a server after approval by the server's owner.

The relationship between your Discord account and your Wikimedia account will not be automatically available or revealed to regular queries on new servers you join. Servers that use WikiAuthBot2 will not know of this relationship until you authenticate via /auth to the specific server.

Access to your information

Besides regular queries, your information are available to developers of WikiAuthBot2, which are bound by the Wikimedia Cloud Services Terms of Use for Administrators and Developers. The information may also be available to the Wikimedia Foundation, volunteer administrators, or other WMCS developers.
